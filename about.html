About The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Understanding our commitment to quality, continuous improvement, and institutional excellence at KIIT Deemed to be University.

Our Vision

To ensure continuous improvement in the entire operations of the institution and assure stakeholders — students, parents, employers, funding agencies, and society at large — of the accountability of the institution for its own quality and probity.

Our Mission

  • To develop a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.
  • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.
  • To optimize and integrate modern methods of teaching and learning.
  • To ensure the adequacy, maintenance, and proper allocation of support structure and services.

Core Objectives of IQAC

Catalytic Improvement

To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.

Quality Culture

To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.

Teaching-Learning

To ensure the optimization and integration of modern methods of teaching and learning, and promote research culture.

Support Systems

To ensure the adequacy, maintenance and functioning of the support structure and services for students, faculty and staff.

Documentation

To ensure proper documentation of the various programmes / activities leading to quality improvement.

Feedback & Action

To establish credible systems for feedback from students, alumni, and other stakeholders on quality-related institutional processes and act upon it.
