About KIIT Deemed to be University

A leading institution dedicated to academic excellence, research, and holistic development.

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) Deemed to be University, located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India, is a globally recognized institution. Established in 1992 as an industrial training institute, it was granted university status in 2004. KIIT is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, diverse academic programs, and a strong focus on research and innovation.

With over 40,000 students pursuing undergraduate and postgraduate courses across various disciplines, KIIT has emerged as a center of excellence. The university is also deeply committed to social responsibility, exemplified by its sister institution, Kalinga Institute of Social Sciences (KISS).
